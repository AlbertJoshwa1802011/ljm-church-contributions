## Formal Announcement

---

**Subject: LJM Church Contributions Portal - Now Live!**

Dear LJM Church Family,

We are excited to announce the launch of our **LJM Church Contributions Portal** - a transparent and user-friendly platform to track and view all contributions to our church funds.

🌐 **Website:** https://light-of-jesus-ministry-contributions.pages.dev/

### Features:
- **Real-time tracking** of Tech Fund and Christmas Fund contributions
- **Personal dashboard** to view your individual contributions
- **Transparent reporting** with detailed statistics and charts
- **Mobile-friendly** design for easy access on any device

### How to Contribute:

**For LJM Members:**
Please send your contributions to me at my mobile number or to **Sugustin Raja**.

**For CWC Members:**
Please send your contributions to **Augustin**.

### How to Use the Portal:

1. Visit the website: https://light-of-jesus-ministry-contributions.pages.dev/
2. Click on **"Funds"** to view Tech Fund or Christmas Fund dashboards
3. Click on **"Members"** to find your name and view your personal contribution history
4. All contributions are updated in real-time and displayed transparently

### Benefits:
- ✅ See your contribution history at a glance
- ✅ Track progress toward fund goals
- ✅ View all contributors and their contributions
- ✅ Access detailed statistics and insights

We encourage all members to explore the portal and see how your faithful giving is making a difference in our church community.

Thank you for your continued support and generosity.

**May God bless you abundantly!**

---

*LJM Church Contributions Team*
